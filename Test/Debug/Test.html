Test: Debug-Class

What

Test the filtering and routing of debug-information to various channels and their formatting

How

  • Open the debug console and press the start button
  • Observe output to the console, the alert window and the textfield

Expect

  • Messages appear in the output targets
  • Message format is [type] | [targets] | [number of argument objects] | [argument objects]
  • Messages appear in the targets listet in [targets]
  • Messages are formatted, formating depends on target, e.g. no groups in alert
